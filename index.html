01 — Who I Am

Me & My Family

My love for Chicano culture started at a young age. I grew up flipping through Lowrider magazines, admiring the artwork, the cars, the bikes, the music — everything that made our culture unique. I spent countless hours drawing little cholo characters, and when the Homies collectibles came out, olvídate — I was hooked. Like many youngsters, I ran with the homies, got into trouble, and experienced the streets firsthand. Those years became part of my story, but they didn't define my future.

02 — My Roots

Culture & Identity

The biggest reason was my father. He never gave up on me. Even when we argued, he kept trying to steer me away from that life. He believed I was capable of more — taught me discipline, respect, and the value of earning an honest living. As I got older, I grew out of the mocoso stage and into the man I wanted to become. Today, I'm a husband, a father, a hardworking man, and an entrepreneur. But one thing never changed — my love for Chicano culture.

03 — The Why

Why Street Scentz

That's why I created Street Scentz. It's more than an air freshener company — it's a tribute to our raza, our memories, and the culture that helped shape us. From Horchata and Fresas Con Crema to every future collection, each design tells a story through artwork, nostalgia, and culture. My hope is that every time you catch one of our scents, it reminds you of where you came from, the people you love, and the pride of being part of a culture rich with history, family, and heart. The Smell That Hits Home.
